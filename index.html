Our Services

Comprehensive care using modern technology, sterile protocols, and a patient-first approach.

General Dentistry
  • Tooth Cleaning: Cleans teeth using advanced products tested worldwide.
  • Dental Fillings: Repairs cavities and protects the tooth from further decay.
  • Oral Checkup: Early detection of gum and tooth problems before they worsen.
Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Teeth Whitening: Brightens stained teeth for a cleaner, whiter smile.
  • Veneers: Covers chipped or uneven teeth for a smooth appearance.
  • Smile Design: Creates a custom treatment plan based on facial features.
Orthodontics
  • Metal Braces: Straightens teeth and corrects bite alignment over time.
  • Clear Aligners: Transparent trays that gradually move teeth with comfort.
  • Bite Correction: Improves chewing and jaw balance for long-term oral health.
Facial Treatments
  • Hydra Facial: Deep-cleans pores while hydrating and soothing the skin.
  • Acne Care: Controls breakouts and reduces marks with guided treatment.
  • Skin Rejuvenation: Improves tone, texture, and glow with clinical methods.
Laser Procedures
  • Pigmentation Laser: Helps fade dark spots and uneven skin tone.
  • Scar Reduction: Smooths acne scars and improves skin texture.
  • Hair Reduction: Targets hair follicles for long-term hair control.
Skin Consultation
  • Skin Analysis: Identifies your skin type, concerns, and treatment goals.
  • Routine Planning: Creates a daily care routine suited to your skin.
  • Progress Review: Tracks improvements and adjusts treatment as needed.
